OpticWorks uses the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct in the hardwareOS repo. This covers expected behavior, enforcement, and reporting.

Key points from `hardwareOS/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`:

- Be respectful, inclusive, and constructive.
- Harassment and personal attacks are unacceptable.
- Community leaders may remove content and enforce consequences.
- Report issues to `support@optic.works`.
